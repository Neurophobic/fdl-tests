NASA Ames has a great tradition of summer programs - some of which, such as the Space Settlement studies led by Gerard O’neill, have inspired a generation of rocketeers and scientists to imagine the impossible.

Our Summer Program aims to recreate this spirit of possibility and by partnering with NVIDIA, Autodesk and SETI we’re also tapping into that indomitable energy of Silicon Valley that can make these imaginings real.
